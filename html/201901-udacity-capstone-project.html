Blends

Mobile / UX

intro

Blends was made as part of Udacity's Android Developer Nanodegree Program. The app offers users the possibility to search for small, independent coffee shops near their homes (only available in London at the moment). What makes this different from all other coffee shop apps is that the cafes displayed are unique, hippie and offer a great caffeinated beverages for anyone who feels adventurous and wishes to experience new tastes.

Objective: create a unique app experience as my capstone project

Role: create app source code, design the UI

Duration: 3 weeks

Tools used: Android Studio, Adobe XD & Photoshop
